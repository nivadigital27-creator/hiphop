What makes this campaign different?

Kuch Kar India links local artists, colleges, NGOs, governments and citizens into one larger movement. Instead of boring lectures, it uses hip-hop culture, visuals and data to create change people can feel.

Street & campus actions

City-specific anti-drug and mental-health campaigns using ciphers, open mics, murals and workshops.

Artists at the centre

Rappers, singers, dancers, filmmakers and comedians collaborate on content that actually travels.

Data & documentation

Stories, numbers and insights that can be used by educators, NGOs and policymakers across India.

Visual language of the movement

Posters and artwork from PMR releases that feed the energy of Kuch Kar India.
